Why ScubaJuan?

Coral Reef Preservation

We ensure all our Divers take approapriate precautions when approaching the reef.
This means no souvenirs, damage or physical abuse of any kind on the fragile Coral ecosystem.

Small-Group Dives (max 6-8)

With smaller groups, Scuba Diving is a lot more fun and significantly safer.
We like to have at least 1 DiveMaster for every 3 Divers.

Safety First

We always stay close enough to our divers, to ensure their comfort, while far enough to let them explore the reef.
Since we work with small groups, it is easy to stay together and make the most our of every dive.

Best Diving Equipment

We use high-quality Scuba Pro equipment for all our dives.
We give it thorough maintenance after every dive, so you always get optimum performance.
We also sell our gear at wholesale prices!

35+ Years PADI Elite Instructor

With more than half a lifetime spent diving, we know these waters better than anyone else.
We know the best locations and techniques to get the most our of your dive, and encounter the most exotic of underwater creatures.
